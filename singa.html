Here are the other places to visit in Singapore:
• Merlion Park - The iconic Merlion Statue, featuring a lion's head and a fish's body that spews water, is more than a mere tourist spot. It stands as a striking symbol of resilience, progress, and the harmonious fusion of tradition and modernity that characterizes Singapore.
• Singapore flyer - The Singapore Flyer is a massive observation wheel that provides breathtaking 360-degree views of Singapore’s skyline, showcasing iconic landmarks like Marina Bay and even offering glimpses of nearby countries.
• Gardens by the Bay - With its striking Supertrees and dedication to environmental sustainability, Gardens by the Bay showcases innovative biodomes and a rich focus on biodiversity. Visitors can explore lush themed gardens, breathtaking floral displays, and immersive green spaces.
