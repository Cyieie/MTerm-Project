Other places to visit in Egypt
• Great Sphinx of Giza- It is one of the world's oldest and most mysterious monuments, believed to date back over 4,500 years. Carved from a single piece of limestone, it features the body of a lion and the head of a pharaoh, symbolizing strength and wisdom, and continues to captivate historians with its enigmatic origins and purpose.
• Luxor Temple - one of the most well-preserved and majestic temples of ancient Egypt, built over 3,000 years ago to honor the gods and celebrate royal power. Unlike other temples dedicated to deities, Luxor Temple was a center for kingship rituals, where pharaohs were crowned, making it a significant symbol of ancient Egyptian civilization.
• Valley of the Kings - served as the burial site for Egypt’s most powerful pharaohs, including Tutankhamun, for over 500 years. Located near Luxor, this ancient necropolis is famous for its richly decorated tombs, hidden treasures, and well-preserved hieroglyphs that provide insight into Egyptian beliefs about the afterlife.
