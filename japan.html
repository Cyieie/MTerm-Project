Other places to visit in Japan
• Shibuya Shibuya Crossing, also called the Shibuya Scramble Crossing, is renowned as the busiest pedestrian crossing in the world. Thousands of people cross from all directions at once, creating a lively and iconic spectacle.-
• Mount Fuji - Mount Fuji is Japan's tallest mountain and a dormant volcano known for its nearly perfect conical shape. It holds deep cultural and spiritual significance, inspiring art, literature, and pilgrimages.
• Kyoto - Kyoto is the cultural heart of Japan, renowned for its rich history, numerous UNESCO World Heritage Sites, and a stunning array of traditional temples, shrines, gardens, and festivals.
